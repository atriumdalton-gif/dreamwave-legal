Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a dream?

Tap the + New Dream button on the home screen. You can type your dream or tap the microphone icon to record your voice — it will be transcribed automatically.

What does the AI do with my dreams?

Your dream text is sent to an AI model (Claude by Anthropic) which analyzes themes, symbols, and emotions. The results are displayed as your dream analysis. No audio is stored — only the transcribed text.

How do I delete my account?

Go to your Profile screen (tap your avatar icon on the home screen), then scroll down and tap Delete Account. This permanently removes all your dreams and account data.

Why is voice input not working?

Voice input requires microphone and speech recognition permissions. Go to Settings → Privacy → Microphone and Speech Recognition and make sure Dreamwave AI is allowed.

Is my dream data private?

Yes. Your dreams are stored securely and are never shared with other users. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
