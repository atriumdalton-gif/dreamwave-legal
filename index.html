The App

What is Dreamwave AI?

Dreamwave AI is an iOS app that turns your dreams into deep psychological insights. You log your dream when you wake up — by typing or speaking — and the AI instantly analyzes it for hidden symbols, emotional patterns, and subconscious messages.

The more you use it, the smarter it gets. It builds a memory of your dream history and spots patterns you'd never notice on your own — recurring themes, emotional shifts, symbols that keep coming back.

Think of it as a personal subconscious therapist that lives in your pocket and learns everything about your inner world.

"Your dreams are the most honest version of you. Dreamwave helps you finally understand what they're saying."

Who Is It For?

  • People who remember their dreams and wonder what they mean
  • Self-improvement and personal growth enthusiasts
  • Therapy-curious people who want to understand their subconscious
  • Spiritual/manifestation communities interested in dream symbolism
  • Anyone who journals and wants an AI-powered upgrade
  • Students of psychology, dream work, or Jungian analysis

The One-Liner

Dreamwave AI decodes your dreams using artificial intelligence — revealing what your subconscious is trying to tell you.

Features

What the App Does

These are the features creators should highlight. Each one is a potential content angle.

🔍
AI Dream Analysis
Log any dream and get an instant breakdown — symbols identified, emotional themes decoded, a "depth score" from 0-100, and a personalized subconscious message. Powered by Claude AI, one of the most advanced AI models in the world.
🎙️
Voice Logging
Speak your dream the moment you wake up. The app transcribes it automatically — so you capture every detail before it fades. Takes 30 seconds.
💬
AI Dream Companion
Chat with an AI that knows your entire dream history. Ask it "why do I keep dreaming about water?" or "what's my biggest fear based on my dreams?" It remembers everything.
📊
Pattern Recognition
The app tracks your recurring symbols, emotions, and themes over weeks and months. You'll start seeing connections between your dreams and your waking life.
📈
Subconscious Reports
Get a weekly AI-generated report that synthesizes all your dreams into a psychological profile — what your mind is processing, emotional trajectories, and growth directions.
🧩
Decode Your Dream
A guided multi-step flow that helps you reconstruct your dream piece by piece — setting, characters, emotions, symbols — before the AI analyzes it. Great for people who only remember fragments.
🔥
Dream Streaks
Log a dream every day to build your streak. The app sends a gentle morning reminder at your wake time. Gamification meets self-discovery.
🔒
Fully Private
Dreams are deeply personal. Everything is encrypted, never shared, never used for training. Users can delete all their data at any time.

Key Talking Points

  • It uses real AI (Claude by Anthropic) — not generic horoscope stuff
  • Voice logging means you capture dreams before they fade
  • The AI companion remembers ALL your dreams — it knows your patterns better than you do
  • The depth score is addictive — people want to see how "deep" their dreams go
  • It's like having a therapist that only analyzes your dreams
  • The more days in a row you log, the more accurate the patterns get
  • Weekly reports feel like getting a psychological profile of yourself
  • It's completely private — your dreams are never shared or used for training

What NOT to Say

  • Don't say it replaces therapy or provides medical advice
  • Don't make health claims — it's for entertainment and self-reflection
  • Don't say "it reads your mind" — it analyzes the dream text you give it
  • Don't share your full login credentials on camera
