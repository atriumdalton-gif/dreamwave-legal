Privacy Policy

Dreamwave AI  ·  Last updated: February 2026

Dreamwave AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use the App:

  • Account information: your email address when you sign up
  • Dream journal entries: the dream descriptions and notes you record in the App
  • Voice recordings: when you use voice input, audio is processed locally on your device using Apple's Speech Recognition framework and converted to text. We do not store audio recordings.
  • AI analysis data: dream text is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate interpretations and insights. See Section 4 for details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To store and sync your dream journal across sessions
  • To generate AI-powered dream analysis, forecasts, and companion insights
  • To provide streak tracking and personalized statistics
  • To display App Store review prompts at milestone moments

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your account and dream data are stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Dream journal content is associated with your account and is not shared with other users.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — authentication and database storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Anthropic (Claude API) — AI analysis of dream content. Dream text is sent to Anthropic to generate interpretations. Anthropic may use this data in accordance with their Privacy Policy. We do not send your name or email to Anthropic.
  • Apple Speech Recognition — on-device transcription of voice input. Governed by Apple's privacy policy.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from the Profile screen inside the App. Deleting your account permanently removes all dream entries and account data from our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

Dreamwave AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To export or request deletion of your data, use the in-app account deletion feature or contact us at the address below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
